All About Monkeys

Overview

Monkeys are a species of monkey found all over the world! They have no whiskers and are usually pretty intelligent.

Species

There are many different kinds of monkey, ranging from spider monkeys to big monkeys to Old World monkeys.

Popular Media

Monkeys are common in popular media today, such as Curious George which is a monkey in a book.

Conclusion
